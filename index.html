Objective: Try to get a hand value as close to 21 as possible without going over. If your hand value exceeds 21, you lose the round (bust).


Instructions:

1. Number cards (2-10) are worth their face value.

2. Face cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10 points.

3. Aces can be worth 1 or 11 points, depending on which value is better for your hand.

4. Hit: Draw another card to improve your hand.

5. Stand: Keep your current hand and end your turn.


Rules:

1. If neither you nor the computer busts, the hand closest to 21 wins.

2. If both have the same value, it's a tie (push).

3. A hand with an Ace and a 10-point card is a Blackjack.

4. Winning with an automatic Blackjack pays 3:2.

5. Winning without a Blackjack pays 1:1.

6. A tie results in no payout.


Place your bets before the game starts where bets can be adjusted each round and winnings are added to your bank!


After each round, you can choose to play again and your bank will reset. Alternatively, you may choose to retain your bank balance by clicking next round.
